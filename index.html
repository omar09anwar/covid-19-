how to stay from covid 19

made by omar anwar.

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30 seconds will help you avoid germs

Cover Your Mouth & Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing in public. Even better use a mask.

Stay Home Stay Safe Avoid

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places. Prefer staying indoors.

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming junk food. Eat as healthy as possible to boost your immunity.

Stay Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a great way to keep your self calm and composed.
